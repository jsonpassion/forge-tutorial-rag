# Privacy Policy — Forge: RAG Essential

> Last Updated: 2026-03-16

---

## English

### Overview

Forge: RAG Essential ("the App") is an educational tutorial app developed by Jason (iamjason-bu). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

### Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App does not require account creation, login, or any form of registration. All tutorial content is bundled within the App and available offline.

### iCloud Sync

The App uses Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) to sync the following data across your devices:

- Reading progress (which sections you have read)
- Bookmarks / Favorites

This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is **not accessible to the developer**. Apple's iCloud is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Analytics & Tracking

The App does **not** include any third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, behavior, or reading patterns.

### Third-Party Services

The App does not communicate with any external servers. All content is available offline once installed.

### Children's Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

### Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

- GitHub: [github.com/jsonpassion](https://github.com/jsonpassion)

본 앱은 외부 서버와 통신하지 않습니다. 설치 후 모든 콘텐츠를 오프라인으로 이용할 수 있습니다.
